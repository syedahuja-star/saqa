Individual Verification Letters

This service is for individuals who need a verification letter confirming their tertiary qualifications obtained in South Africa.

The verification letter is limited to registered qualifications and NOT short courses nor foreign qualifications or professional designations.

To access this service click on the links and send your request to verificationsletter@saqa.org.za with the subject “Verification Letter – Quotation”.

NOTE:
Please note that all verification letter requests and enquiries must always be forwarded to verificationsletter@saqa.org.za. Requests sent to any other e-mail address will not be attended to.

Please also note that the turnaround time regarding an outcome to your application is a minimum of 25 working days. Please also be on the lookout for possible responses to your application, should it not be compliant for processing and further requirements (check your email spam folders too).
